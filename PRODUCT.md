# Product

## Register

product

## Users

VeriDoc is for document-conversion operators and reviewers who need to inspect PDF, Word, and Excel conversion output before it can be trusted downstream. They work in a review context where source position, extracted text, validation warnings, and approval or correction decisions need to stay traceable.

## Product Purpose

VeriDoc converts and reconstructs regulated or quality-sensitive documents into structured document IR. Success means reviewers can compare the source document with conversion output, focus on items that require review, correct or approve findings, and produce audit-ready events for later logging.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, operational. The interface should feel like a workbench for careful review rather than a marketing surface.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style hero layouts, decorative visual effects, and playful copy. Avoid UI that hides provenance, separates findings from source position, or treats review decisions as informal notes.

## Design Principles

- Keep source provenance adjacent to every review decision.
- Prefer explicit blocked or rejected states over inferred success.
- Make repeated review work dense enough to scan without losing traceability.
- Preserve audit intent in labels, controls, and event payloads.
- Keep the visual system restrained so document evidence remains primary.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic controls, visible status text, keyboard-accessible review actions, and contrast appropriate for sustained document review. Avoid motion or visual treatment that makes source/result comparison harder.
